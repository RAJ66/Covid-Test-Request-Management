Welcome to Centro Regional de Análises

This iniciative seeks to help the local government control the spread of the COVID-19, and improve the efficiency of the scheduling of the tests. The way it works is very simple, below you have the flow of how you should expect the system will work.

How it works

The Creation of the Request

In order to you create a request, in the top of the page, you have a section where you can create a request. It's a simple form where you only have to answer 3 questions.

Check the Requests

After your request is submited, you can check all your requests and also check all the details of the requests here.

The Treatment of the Request

After you request is submited, our employees have now access to your request and one of them will take care of it.

The Scheduling of the Tests

After one of our employees claims your requests, he/she will be the one to schedule the date of the first test.

Second Test

In case the first tests result comes negative, the date for the second test is automatically schedule, and you will receive an e-mail.

The Updates

After one of our employees claims your requests, he/she will now update your request and when he/she does, you will receive an e-mail about that update.

And always make sure you get you information about the COVID-19 in a reliable information source, such as:

WHO - World Health Organization

DGS - Direção Geral da Saúde
